Redefining used car buying and selling with Spinny

About Spinny

Spinny is a technology driven used cars buying and selling platform redefining used cars market in India. Their pre-inspected used cars end the information and pricing asymmetry by using innovate rating, pricing and inspection processes. Spinny is an end-to-end solution that provides both buyers and sellers with a transparent, safe and convenient way of transacting used cars.

My Role

I led the design with the crazy telented team at Spinny. From collaboration with the super-developers to operation managers, I handled and paved the path for the entire product design. I designed multiple products including internal CRM and apps to end-user (human) experiences.

Spinning the timeline a little backwards...

When I joined Spinny, the end user website was way ahead in terms of experience than most of the competition in the online used car buying and selling space, the internal apps and CRM however, lacked a lot design-wise. It had been 8 months since the inception of public face of Spinny and they hired as a UX Designer to understand the users better and come up with fast beautiful solutions.

First look at the dashboard and I had already put a knife in my eye for its absolute brutal use of eye catching red all over the interface. This was a pure developer's solution to problems, crafted by Ramanshu (CTO & Co-Founder, Spinny). He was desperate to get a better version out in tight deadlines too.
