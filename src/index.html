If we do a project together, it is important for you to understand how I work. I believe that design is not simply put on top of an application but a process. And this is how I prefer to make products. [ View Details ]

Define Product

In the initial phase, brainstorm the product with stakeholders and define how to execute the project via a kickoff plan, lean canvas or really early concept map.

Research

Refine the assumptions and based on complexity of product, timing and other factors, conduct surveys and analyse to fill in the blanks of the canvas.

Analyze

The data collected during research lays the foundation for personas, scenarios, experience maps and defines project priorities for intermediate and final deliverables.

Design

Using the concept maps, scenarios etc. document the intermediate deliverables such as sketches, wireframes, storyboards, prototypes and design specs.

Implement

The prototypes lead to delivery of high fedility mockups, design and code assets, styleguides etc. Supporting the developers is the prime focus here.

Test and Iterate

Feedback data through bug reports, performance and analytics dashboards are then used continually to acheive data driven product improvement.
